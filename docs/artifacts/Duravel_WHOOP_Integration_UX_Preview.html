How Duravel uses WHOOP data

Duravel builds and continuously adapts personalized race-prep plans for HYROX, DEKA, and triathlon athletes. A member connects WHOOP once; from then on their daily recovery, HRV, resting heart rate, sleep, and strain flow into the plan — easing intensity on low-recovery days and progressing load when recovery supports it. How Duravel handles WHOOP data

  • WHOOP data is used solely to power the member's own training experience — it is never sold and never used for advertising.
  • Access is member-initiated via OAuth and requested at the minimum scope needed for readiness and activity history.
  • Members can disconnect at any time; disconnecting immediately stops ingestion and deletes the WHOOP data Duravel holds for that member.
  • WHOOP data is not shared with any third party, consistent with the WHOOP Developer Terms.
